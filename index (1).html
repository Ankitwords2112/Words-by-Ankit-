Shayari #1 - Tere Bina

Tere bina zindagi adhuri si lagti hai,
Jaise khushbu ke bina koi phool ho.
Har pal teri yaadon mein kho jaata hoon,
Tere bina main bilkul akela hoon.

Shayari #2 - Yaadon Ka Safar

Raat ke sannate mein tujhe sochta hoon,
Har pal teri yaadon mein kho jaata hoon,
Zindagi ek kitaab si ban gayi hai,
Jisme har panna sirf tera naam likhta hoon.
